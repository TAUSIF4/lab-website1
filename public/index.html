Our Diagnostic Services

Accurate, reliable, and convenient testing you can trust. Explore our wide range of tests designed for your health and well-being.

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

A comprehensive test that screens for a variety of disorders like anemia and infection.

Thyroid Profile

Measures TSH, T3, and T4 hormones to assess thyroid function and related disorders.

Diabetic Profile

Includes HbA1c and Fasting Blood Sugar to monitor and manage diabetes.

Assesses the health and function of your liver by measuring key enzymes and proteins.
